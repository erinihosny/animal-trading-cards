Hamster

Hamsters can store food in special pouches in their cheeks and they have a great sense of smell, but not great eyesight.

  • Scientific name: Cricetinae
  • Classification: Kingdom: Animalia , Phylum: Chordata , Class: Mammalia , Order: Rodentia , Family: Cricetidae.
  • Mass: They usually weigh from 17 to 45g
  • Habitat: Syria

Hamsters are the most popular of the small pets. They are very clever and adventurous little animals and deserve consideration, care and commitment from their owner..
